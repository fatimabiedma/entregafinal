ABOUT ME

Fátima Biedma

Hello! I'm Fátima Biedma. I am a Graphic Designer since 2019. I Graduated in Buenos Aires University (UBA) with 22 years old. Actually I have 24 years old.

I've been working as freelancer designer. I also provide solutions to companies so that the message they want to communicate transcends.

My goal as a designer is to communicate, to facilitate the user experience when facing the world. Where there are multiple looks / sensations / emotions, and I stop at each one of them.

Through a deep analysis of the brand where its objective, its competencies are taken into account and from that analysis the creation of communication pieces. Branding, packaging, graphic design, social media, audiovisual pieces, with their own unique identity.
